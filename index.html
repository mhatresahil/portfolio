Algorithmic Trading Bot

Algorithmic trading employs algorithms that track a trend and create a set of instructions for executing a trade. The Algorithmic Trading BOT allows the user to do 1. Live Trading 2. Paper Trading (Live Simulation Trading) 3. Back-Testing of Strategies Developed using technologies like PostgreSQL database, Python 3.7, Django, Alpaca Trading and Paper Trading Account

TRUPPT-Vocal for Local Initiative

TRUPPT uses AI to help small and medium-sized businesses access national and global markets by connecting suppliers with buyers and organisations. It may also help lower unemployment. This user-friendly technology that gives multilingual support in several languages may benefit people from various socioeconomic backgrounds. Developed using technologies like Python, Django, Postgres, HTML, JavaScript

QR Based Voting App

The development of a website is to help the electoral authority in the organisation and administration of elections. Voters may cast their votes quickly and simply after being verified (liveness and face detection + QR Code).  Using Python, OpenCV, Django, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQLLite, and TensorFlow.

Food Express

Multiple companies may manage their own menus and orders using this online application, which was designed to be self-sufficient. Customers are provided an easy-to-use cart that manages the goods, quantities, and total cost of the order from the restaurant of their choice. HTML, CSS, JS, and Django are the technologies utilised.

Airline Reservation System

Users may book flight tickets, check seat availability, view the most popular destinations throughout the world, and check the weather conditions of desired destination before reservations using this website. Developed using Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
